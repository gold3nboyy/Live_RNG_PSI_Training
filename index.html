How & Why

How To Use: This generator produces a continuous stream of random numbers based on your chosen range and speed. You can start or stop it at any moment, and the newest number always appears on the left side of the history. It does not use seeds, so it never starts from a fixed point or repeats a preset pattern. Instead, it uses the device’s hardware to create randomness fresh every time — like rolling a real die inside the phone or computer.

Intention & Psi Training: Research in parapsychology, including work at SRI and later laboratories, has explored whether human intention or psi abilities can subtly bias random systems. Results are mixed and debated, but many people use random number generators as a way to train focus, intuition, and mind–matter interaction. This generator gives you a clean, unbiased target for your own practice. You can focus on influencing the numbers toward a chosen outcome and then use the statistics to see whether, over many trials, your results differ from what chance alone would produce.

Detailed Explanations

History (Last 100 Numbers): Shows the most recent 100 values. Colors mark streaks: red for single values, yellow for pairs, green for longer streaks.

Entropy: Measures how widely values are spread. The word below the value indicates whether the spread suggests no psi effect, slight effect, significant effect, or strong effect.

Chi² p-value: Shows how close the distribution is to uniform randomness. The word below the value summarizes the psi effect strength.

Deviation: Shows how far the distribution differs from uniform randomness. The word below the percentage indicates psi effect strength.

Heatmap: Displays the ten most frequent numbers from the last 100 results.

Chart: Shows the last 80 generated numbers as a continuous line.

Hope & Feedback: This tool is designed for people who are genuinely curious about psi, ESP, and mind–matter interaction. It does not promise results, but it gives you clear feedback. If you see patterns that go beyond chance, you can explore them. If you do not, you still gain practice in focus and self-observation.

Tip: You can start or stop the generator by pressing the spacebar or tapping the big number.
